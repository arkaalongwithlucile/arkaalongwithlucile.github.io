About Arka

Arka is a dynamic musician, composer, and arranger with a passion for diverse musical expressions. Often collaborating with the talented contemporary artists, bringing a unique depth to the projects.

A prominent figure in the music scene, Arka is an integral part of acclaimed bands like "Gulmo" and "Anwesha and the Alternates". His collaborative spirit extends to various other artists and projects, showcasing his versatility.

With a keen ear for emotive storytelling through music, Arka has composed and produced background scores (BGM) for OTT releases, feature films, and television serials. His expertise also includes crafting memorable commercial arrangements and exploring innovative sounds through experimental projects and covers.
